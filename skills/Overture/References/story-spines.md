# story-spines.md
> Overture · reference file 01
> Loaded at Step 3. The approved narrative structures. One per asset.
> Every spine is 3-act compressed to seconds; the viewer is always the hero.

---

## S-01 · The Correction
- **Shape:** wrong answer appears → is rejected → true answer earns its place
- **Setup / Tension / Resolution:** generic claim / the strike-through / the
  specific claim standing alone
- **brand.md trace:** Rejects — "willing to reject a weak answer and ask again"
- **Natural devices:** D1 (strike-through), D8 (margin annotation)
- **Best placements:** hero-loop, episode-open
- **Note:** this is the page's signature moment (detail-patterns P-05).
  Off-hero use of this spine must not reuse the strike-through device.

## S-02 · The Derivation
- **Shape:** a foundation is laid → construction proceeds visibly → the result
  carries its own proof
- **Setup / Tension / Resolution:** blank paper + first guide line / lines
  accumulating, one attempt struck / the finished form with construction
  still visible
- **brand.md trace:** derivation over decoration; auditability
- **Natural devices:** D2 (construction), D5 (registration cross)
- **Best placements:** demo, section-motion (How It Works)

## S-03 · The Cost of the True Answer
- **Shape:** many attempts → most fail visibly → one survives
- **Setup / Tension / Resolution:** the grid of attempts / sequential
  cross-outs / the circle around the survivor
- **brand.md trace:** belief shift — "slow enough to find the true answer"
- **Natural devices:** D4 (proof sheet — the only device allowed an extended
  sequence; duration IS the meaning here)
- **Best placements:** demo, episode-open, What You Get section-motion

## S-04 · Before / After (StoryBrand transformation)
- **Shape:** founder's before-state → encounter with the question → after-state
- **Setup / Tension / Resolution:** the template-tweaking founder / "why are
  we here" asked and pushed back / the founder with an answer they believe
- **brand.md trace:** Purpose — "a forced, honest answer to 'why are we here'"
- **Natural devices:** D8 (margin annotation carries the question), D3
  (half-cut stencil as identity mid-emergence)
- **Best placements:** teaser, episode-open
- **Guard:** the after-state is the founder's clarity, never the product's
  interface. Product stays the guide.

## S-05 · In Medias Res
- **Shape:** open inside the tension (mid-correction, mid-cut, mid-session) →
  brief rewind or context → resolve forward
- **brand.md trace:** the experience of being asked is itself the differentiator
- **Natural devices:** any device caught mid-action; D3 mid-cut is canonical
- **Best placements:** teaser, episode-open (cold-open native)
- **Guard:** the rewind must be one beat, not a second act.

---

## Selection rule
Match the placement's `section_argument` first (critique → S-01/S-03; method
→ S-02; soul → S-04/S-05; personality → any, told deadpan), then choose the
spine whose natural devices are inside the page's device budget. If no spine
fits the budget, the placement may not need motion — R0 is a valid answer.
