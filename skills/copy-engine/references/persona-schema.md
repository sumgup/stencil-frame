# Persona Schema — Template for New Personas

Use this template when creating a new persona from a co-thinking session
(Step 1) or from VoC research. Fill what you know, mark what you don't.
Every field should note its source and confidence.

Target length: 500-700 words. Must be AI-ingestible (paste into a system
prompt without preprocessing).

---

---

## Rules for good personas

1. VoC quotes > paraphrases. Always. The customer's words are the most
   valuable field in this document.
2. "Draft" status means hypothesis. Only mark "validated" after 5+
   interviews confirm the core JTBD and pain points.
3. Every field should have a source. "Guess" is a valid source — just
   label it honestly.
4. Update after every research touchpoint. Bump the date. Note what changed.
5. Merge two personas if their actionable fields overlap >70%.
6. Split a persona if awareness stage or primary job forks by channel.
7. Retire (archive, don't delete) if unused for 3+ months.
