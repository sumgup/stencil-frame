# IA Problem Statement

Status: DRAFT — to be validated with founder sessions

## The three layers

### Layer 1: "Where am I?" (session wayfinding)
A founder entering the Stencil session needs to understand where they
are in the process at all times. Five acts is a lot. The session is
emotional (Act 0 is introspective, Act 2b is revelatory). If they lose
orientation, the emotional arc breaks.

Open question: Is the five-label progress indicator (Who/Warm/Research/
Map/Confirm) sufficient, or does the founder need more wayfinding?

### Layer 2: "What did I get?" (output comprehension)
After Act 3, brand.md is written. But brand.md is a YAML file. The
founder needs to understand what they received, what each section means,
and what they can do with it.

Open question: How do we present brand.md as something the founder
values and understands, not a config file?

### Layer 3: "What happens next?" (Stencil → Frame transition)
The founder completes Stencil and then... what? Frame doesn't exist yet
in v0. The transition from "I discovered my positioning" to "I can make
things from it" is the biggest IA gap.

Open question: What is the minimum viable "next step" after Stencil
completes? Even if Frame isn't built yet?

## Validation plan
These problems are hypotheses. The concierge founder sessions (target:
5 sessions) will validate or reshape them. After sessions, update this
doc with real observations.
