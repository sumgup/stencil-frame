## STRATEGY DISPOSITION — SEALED 2026-07-24
- **Committed:** Stencil to launch + concierge sessions + YouTube channel. Nothing else builds.
- **brand.md = the open standard.** Auditability is its law. Any generator may render it.
- **Frame = gated question, not promise.** Build-vs-integrate decided ONLY at the five-session gate, with founder evidence. Not reopenable by mood.
- **Name: KEPT — "Stencil + Frame,"** re-read as one printmaking tool. Canonical one-liner:
  "In screen printing, the frame isn't decoration — it's what holds the stencil taut and in
  register, so the mark comes out identical every single pull. The stencil is your positioning —
  you cut it once, carefully. The frame is what holds it steady, so every asset you ever print
  comes out in register with it."
  "Frame" names a FUNCTION (whatever holds brand.md in register during generation), so the name
  is honest even if Frame-the-product never ships. Registration = auditability, in printmaker's language.
- **Usage rule:** "S&F" is casual shorthand only. Official surfaces use the full name or "Stencil."
- **Logo: KEPT — Vesica Piscis,** meaning re-anchored to IDENTITY ∩ GAP ("gap without identity is
  capitalism; identity without gap is noise; both must coexist"). Product-architecture-independent.
  P0.9 finalization proceeds after current run closes.
- **Name field-test (cheap, optional):** during the S/C/S+ audience test, give half the listeners the
  one-liner and check they can repeat the name's story back five minutes later.

## Process rules (standing)
1. Locked decisions reopen only when their underlying evidence changes.
2. Never finalize a style choice same-day as its capture session (primed taste ≠ taste).
3. Multiple visual languages coexist only under one governing concept.
4. Idiom carries meaning, not hue — judge executions, not swatches.
5. Trend literacy note: record what trends signify + react against.
6. Claude does not design ahead of the art director.
7. New ideas mid-process → inspiration queue.
