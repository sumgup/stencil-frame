## Anti-procrastination rules
1. **One mode per session.** Builder, Artist, or Entrepreneur. Never all three.
2. **Build one visible thing per session.** If you can't see it at the end, the session failed.
3. **Commit and push at the end of every build session.** No local-only work.
4. **Milestone review every two weeks.** 30 minutes. Update this document. Adjust dates.
5. **YouTube deadline is non-negotiable.** Week 8. The video exists — it just needs to be recorded.

---

## What NOT to do (common ADHD traps)
- Do not redesign Act 0 again until Act 1–3 are built
- Do not add features before existing ones are tested
- Do not wait for perfect illustrations before building
- Do not delay YouTube until the product is "ready enough" — it never will be
- Do not start Frame until Stencil Investigate is complete
