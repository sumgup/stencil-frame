# Stencil + Frame — Brand Book copy

Generated by Frame's copywriting SKILL v0, reading brand.md. First real
output of the engine aimed at itself — Session Zero, Task 5.

## Tagline (final)

Positioning is the hero.

*(Technique: Ogilvy — the thesis as the headline, no category descriptor.
Fallback for cold/non-technical audiences, if ever needed: "Why you exist
is the hero.")*

## Hero line (final)

Brands have a color palette where a reason to exist should be.

*(Technique: Schwartz — meets the problem-aware, not-yet-solution-aware
founder at the symptom, not the fix. Revised from draft to cut the
softening qualifier "Most brands" — the claim is stronger without it.)*

## Manifesto paragraph (final)

Founders are handed shortcuts to visuals when what they need is a forced,
honest answer to why they exist. The logo generator, the archetype quiz,
the mood board — these all assume the hardest question is already answered.
It isn't. Stencil asks it, waits for a real answer, and won't accept an
industry-worded version of nothing — including from itself. Everything
else — voice, visual direction, content — derives from that answer.

*(Technique: Wiebe — written to the emotional job, not the functional one.)*

## Waitlist CTA (final)

**Live CTA:** Join the waitlist — we'll ask you why you exist.

**Saved, not live** — "Get in line." Genuinely on-voice flat-delivery dark
humor (double reading: waitlist queue / submit to the process), but relies
on the reader catching the second meaning, which is a real risk for a cold
first-time visitor. Held for social posts, merch, or an easter egg —
somewhere the audience already knows the brand's voice.

## Boilerplate (final)

Stencil + Frame is an open-source brand engine built on the thesis that
positioning precedes everything else. Stencil is a guided, multi-step
discovery — willing to reject a weak answer and ask again — that produces
a structured brand.md file as the actual deliverable. Frame reads that
file and generates voice direction, visual guidance, and content from it.
The system is AI-agnostic: any LLM can power it; brand.md is the contract.
